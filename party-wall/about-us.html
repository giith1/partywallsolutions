About Party Wall Solutions

At Party Wall Solutions, we specialize in providing expert guidance and support in navigating the complexities of the Party Wall Act. Our dedicated team of experienced surveyors is committed to ensuring that property owners, whether involved in building projects or dealing with issues related to adjoining properties, receive clear, reliable, and proactive advice.

The Party Wall Act is a key piece of legislation designed to resolve disputes and prevent potential conflicts between property owners concerning shared walls (party walls), boundaries, and structures. It lays out a clear framework for building works and renovations that may impact a neighboring property. However, understanding and complying with the Act can often be a daunting task, and that's where Party Wall Solutions steps in.

What We Do

We offer a range of services to both building/property owners and adjoining property owners, helping them navigate their rights and responsibilities under the Party Wall Act. Whether you're embarking on a construction project or need guidance on a notice you've received, our team is here to help.

For Building/Property Owners, we provide expert advice to help determine if your project requires a Party Wall Notice. We are dedicated to offering honest, clear, and transparent guidance on all matters relating to the Party Wall Act, ensuring compliance and minimizing the risk of disputes with neighboring properties. Our fixed-fee services mean that you know exactly what to expect from the outset, without any unexpected costs. We also leverage modern technology to streamline communication and reduce delays, ensuring your project moves forward smoothly and efficiently.

For Adjoining Property Owners, we provide expert support in responding to Party Wall Notices. If you've been served with a notice or are concerned about construction works next door, we are here to help. Our experienced surveyors will guide you through the process and advise you on your options, ensuring you understand your rights and how to respond. We also offer advice for situations where work has begun without the appropriate notice, helping you take the necessary steps to protect your interests.
